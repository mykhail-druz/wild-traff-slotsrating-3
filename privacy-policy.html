Overview

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us provide and improve our services. We use both session cookies (temporary) and persistent cookies (stored longer-term).

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand website usage
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Marketing Cookies: Deliver relevant advertisements

Cookie Management

You can control cookies through:

  • Your browser settings to block or delete cookies
  • Our cookie consent banner on first visit
  • Cookie preference center (link in footer)
  • Opt-out links for third-party services

Cookie Notice

Some cookies are essential for website functionality. Disabling these may affect your ability to use certain features of our website.
